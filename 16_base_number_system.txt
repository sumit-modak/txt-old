7-segment display based 
    vertical    horizontal
0 ~ bottom      left 
1 ~ top         right

00 = bottom_left
01 = bottom_right
10 = top_left
11 = top_right

10 -> 00    top_left -> bottom_left
10 -> 01    top_left -> bottom_right
11 -> 00    top_right -> bottom_left
11 -> 01    top_right -> bottom_right

 _         _    _   
| |  |_    _|    |  
|_|   _|  |     _|
